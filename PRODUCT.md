# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, recruiters, engineering peers, and collaborators reviewing Temoso's work from a desktop or mobile browser. They are scanning for credibility, taste, communication clarity, and a quick way to contact him.

## Product Purpose

This personal portfolio introduces Temoso as a Software Engineer in Cape Town, South Africa, presents his about copy, skills, projects, writing, and contact paths, and makes it easy to evaluate whether to reach out about opportunities.

## Brand Personality

Clean, technical, intentional. The interface should feel confident and polished without adding invented claims, services, testimonials, or inflated language.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic portfolio templates, AI-purple gradient pages, overstuffed card grids, fake testimonials, stock marketing copy, and decorative effects that distract from the content. The codedgar.com reference is structural inspiration only: numbered sections, terminal-style labels, clean cards, and strong hierarchy.

## Design Principles

1. Preserve the real content and make it easier to scan.
2. Let typography, spacing, and contrast carry the premium feel.
3. Use the orange-red accent sparingly so it stays meaningful.
4. Make dark mode the primary experience and light mode equally resolved.
5. Add motion only where it clarifies state, hierarchy, or focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible color contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, semantic landmarks, useful alt text, and reduced-motion support for all animation.
